## Overview

Organize all your project docs by using folders and tags.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new folder">
    ````
    On your dashboard, click "New Folder" and enter a name—e.g., "Website Redesign".
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documentation">
    ````
    Click inside the folder, then "New Doc" to start creating content for your project.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply tags">
    ````
    Use tags like "UI", "Backend", or "2024" to make searching easier.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
