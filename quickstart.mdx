## Overview

Get started with José María Álvarez in minutes. Follow these steps to create an account, set up a new documentation space, add your first page, and explore basic navigation and editing. No prior experience required—you'll have a live documentation site ready to share.

<Callout kind="info">
**Prerequisites:**
- A valid email address
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- 5 minutes of your time
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up quickly to access your documentation dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Navigate to `https://app.josemariaalvarez.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, full name, and a secure password. Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard and sign in with your credentials. You'll land on the main workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create a New Documentation Space

Organize your projects in dedicated spaces.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space**. Enter a name like "My Project Docs".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Basics" icon="settings">
    Select a brand color (e.g., `#3B82F6`) and add a description: "Welcome to my project documentation."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Space" icon="globe">
    Click **Create**. Your space is now live at a unique URL like `https://docs.josemariaalvarez.com/my-project`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Your First Page

Build content using simple MDX editing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Page" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click **New Page**. Title it "Introduction".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Content" icon="edit-3">
    Use the built-in editor to add frontmatter and body content. Here's a starter example:
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="Frontmatter,Body">
    ````markdown
    ---
    title: Introduction
    description: Welcome to the docs.
    ---
    
    ## Hello World
    
    Your content starts here.
    ````
    ````
    
    ## Overview
    
    This is your first page.
    
    <Callout kind="tip">
    Save frequently!
    </Callout>
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="eye">
    Click **Preview** to see the rendered page, then **Publish**. Share the live URL.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Basic Navigation and Editing

Master the interface for efficient workflows.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Navigation" icon="menu">
    Use the sidebar for spaces, pages, and settings. Search with `<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd>`. Breadcrumbs show your location.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Editing Tips" icon="edit">
    - Switch to Markdown or Visual mode
    - Add components like `<Callout>` via autocomplete
    - Use version history to revert changes
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Keyboard Shortcuts" icon="keyboard">
    
    | Action          | Shortcut       |
    |-----------------|----------------|
    | New Page        | `<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>N</kbd>` |
    | Save            | `<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>S</kbd>` |
    | Preview         | `<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>P</kbd>` |
    
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
Pro tip: Customize your space sidebar with pinned pages for quick access.
</Callout>
